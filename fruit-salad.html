Fruit Salad

The fruit salad was made during the US Naval Era, in 1900. Fruit salads were often made in the 1920's.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 cup of fresh frozen raspberries
  • 1 cup of grapes
  • 1 cup of diced apples
  • 1 cup of cut bananas
  • 1/2 cup of strawberry or vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of cool whip
  • 1 cup of mini marshmellows

Directions

  1. Get the 1/2 cup and the 1 cup ready
  2. Next you need to get all of the ingredients
  3. Next you have to cut the fruit you need to cut
  4. After that you have to get a bowl does not matter how big it is
  5. Next you put all of the ingredients and mix them but not the whip cream and the marshmellows
  6. Last after you mix you add the marshmellows and the whip cream
